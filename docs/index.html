Find your path

Hunter Odyssey is a game where you find yourself submerged in a world of fantasy, with dangerous enemies and unique puzzles. Play as multiple characters to achieve your goal in this brand new game!

The Hunter Odyssey is a RPG turn-based video game. You are a bounty hunter who has heard rumors about the prince from the most important region of the country, who is supposedly kidnapped by a majestuous monster. So, you decide to visit that famous place, with the objective of discovering all the secrets around this case.

Features

• Fight with enemies in an amazing turn-based combat type!
• Play with keyboard or even a gamepad!
• Play as a Wizard, Hunter, Warrior or a Thief!
• Solve unique and hard puzzles across the dungeon!
